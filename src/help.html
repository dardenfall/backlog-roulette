Your steam profile url is the url for your steam profile (duh) that's visible to the world. If you have a steam account, there is a page on steamcommunity.com with your user. If you haven't already set your personal url, then your page will have a random number inserted for your id. You can use that url or follow the directions below to set up your own.
  1. Go to store.steampowered.com and click the profile dropdown in the upper right. Click view profile.
  2. On your profile page click edit profile
  3. Scroll down to custom url and insert your steam ID or some other string. Don't forget to save
